Mech Industries 2022 – 2023

MechRoyale

High-stakes multiplayer Battle Royale featuring real-time custom mech building and expansive world maps.

Developed at Mech Industries ($2M+ Raised)

MechRoyale puts a unique twist on the battle royale genre by allowing players to construct their own combat machines on the fly. Dropping into a massive, shrinking battlefield, players must scavenge for chassis parts, weapons, and utility modules to assemble the ultimate fighting vehicle while engaging in intense multiplayer combat.

Key Features

  • Real-time Mech Assembly: dynamic customization system allowing players to swap parts instantly.
  • Massive World Map: Designed and optimized large-scale environments with varied biomes.
  • Multiplayer Networking: Server architecture for seamless online matches.
  • Advanced Looting System: Context-sensitive looting and inventory management.
  • Cross-Platform Development: Optimized for performance on both PC and mobile devices.
