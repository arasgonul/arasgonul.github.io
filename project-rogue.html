AI-Native Village Sim — A platformer + village sim where every NPC has a real AI brain.

This project is a platformer and village simulation game where every NPC is powered by a real, local AI brain. You can text any villager, and they respond with context, emotion, and persistent memory — all processed locally on your GPU with zero cloud dependency.

Key Features

  • 100s of AI-Driven NPCs: Each character has unique backstories, moods, jobs, health stats, and relationships.
  • Talk to Anyone: Text any NPC about their past, friends, feelings, or village events. They maintain context indefinitely.
  • Permanent Memory: Characters remember every interaction across saves and long play sessions.
  • Dynamic Personalities: Your words can change their mood, history, or even their identity. You can grant titles through dialogue — e.g. Greg becomes Greg the Ironfist, shown on his nameplate.
  • World-Aware Simulation: NPCs react dynamically to in-game events like floods, disease, caravans, and local gossip.
  • Fully Local Execution: Runs entirely on an NVIDIA GPU with zero external APIs, ensuring zero latency and full privacy.
