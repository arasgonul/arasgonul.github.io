Kalahari Depths & Mech3

The evolution of a Match-3 game: From "Kalahari Depths" (Mech Industries) to the AI-reskinned "Mech3" (MixieAI).

Acquired by Zelgor (NetCapital, NASDAQ: NCPL).

The Evolution

What started as Kalahari Depths—the first minigame for the Mech Industries metaverse (contributing to over $2M raised)—was later evolved into Mech3 at MixieAI.

I led the development of the original game, including core logic, loot box systems, and UI. Later, at MixieAI, I integrated generative AI pipelines to completely reskin the game with new 3D and 2D assets, while also optimizing it for Telegram deployment and adding difficulty progression and power-ups.
